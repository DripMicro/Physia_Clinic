Our Programs

You are one of a kind. And so is your body. Which is why we take a personal approach to online physical therapy. First we get to know you and your expectations, and only then we provide you with a bespoke treatment which you can complete at home.

Physia Clinic world-class physical therapists and neurologists spent years developing treatments for a vast spectrum of people. Whether you are 12 or 90, an office worker or an aspiring athlete, we have a treatment for you.

Show your body some love and start your home-based online physical therapy with Physia Clinic today.

Lower Back

Lower Back

Highly effective with all lower back pain problems such as:

  • Prolapsed (herniated) disk
  • Spondylitis
  • Spondylosis
  • Spondylolisthesis / Retrolisthesis
  • Spinal stenosis
  • Sacroiliac Joint Misalignment
  • Spinal Arthritis
  • Lumbar radiculopathy and more.

From $100/ month.

Neck
